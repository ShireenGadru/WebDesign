GAMEMODES

Payload

In Payload maps, BLU team must escort a cart full of explosives through a series of checkpoints and into RED's base within a certain amount of time. BLU team members move or 'push' the cart by standing next to it â€” the more people next to the cart, the faster it moves.

Capture the Flag

Capture the Flag features both RED and BLU bases, each containing its own Intelligence briefcase. The goal for both teams is to capture the enemy's Intelligence until they have reached the game's capture limit while preventing the enemy team from achieving the same.

Control Point

Control points are circular platforms with a team-colored light and hologram in the center. To capture a point owned by the enemy team, the player simply stands on the point until the capture meter fills with his or her team color.
